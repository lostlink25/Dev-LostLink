Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about LostLink’s lost and found services.

How do I report a lost item?

Visit our Report Lost Items page, fill out the form with details about your item, and submit. Our team will start searching.

Can I track my lost item?

Yes! Use the Track Items page to check real-time updates on your reported item’s status.

How do I claim a found item?

Go to the Claim Items page, browse found items, and submit a claim with verification details. We’ll arrange the return.

What if my item isn’t found?

We’ll keep searching and update you. Contact our support team via Contact Us for further assistance.

Is there a fee for your services?

Reporting and tracking are free. A small fee may apply for item return shipping, depending on location.

How can I contact support?

Reach us 24/7 via phone, email, or live chat through our Contact Us page.

Need Help with a Lost Item?

Contact our 24/7 support team or visit our Lost and Found office at the airport.
