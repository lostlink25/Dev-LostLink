Sign In

Enter your staff credentials to access the LostLink staff portal. As a staff member, you can report found items, update item statuses, verify claims, and communicate with passengers. If you encounter issues, please contact support.

Staff Responsibilities

  • Log and categorize found items in the system.
  • Update the status of lost and found items.
  • Verify passenger claims for found items.
  • Coordinate with passengers for item retrieval.
